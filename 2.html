B.Tech Admissions Open

The university is offering admission in the following B.Tech branches:

  • Computer Science Engineering (CSE)
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

Admission Fees

The total admission fee for B.Tech is approximately 6 Lakhs.

Eligibility Criteria

Students must have at least 75% marks in both 10th and 12th to apply for admission.

Mess / Canteen Fees

Hostel students need to pay additional charges for mess and canteen facilities. The approximate mess and canteen fee is around ₹80,000 per year.

About

Annamacharya University provides quality education in engineering and technology with experienced faculty and modern infrastructure.
